AmpGen::RecursivePhaseSpace Class Reference

Detailed Description

Generates events in the phase space of a decay with one-or-more quasistable products that are further decayed, where these quasistable products are suitably long-lived that their propagators can be assumed to be delta-functions, i.e. particles such as \( \Lambda^0 , {K_\rm{S}}^0 \) etc. Such a process has a natural factorisation into each of these steps. For each of the decays of a quasistable particle, the PhaseSpace generator is used, which is itself based heavily on TGenPhaseSpace.
